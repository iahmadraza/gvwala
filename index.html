What is GVwala ?

It's an early start up with objective to facilitate & bring gift vouchers (GV's) to the door step of Indian corporate clients.

GVwala helps companies to enhance their employee & customer value proposition by offering wide array of GV's which helps large enterprise Or SME's to drive employee rewards Or channel incentive programs to acknowledge the efforts of their outperformers & to keep them motivated.

Our vision & mission is to become first choice of Indian Enterprise clients for GV's & employee rewards programs by offering world class customer experience.

A step towards creating wow customer experience.

How GVwala Works ?

It's simple.

Corporate clients can just rope us in & register us as vendor / supplier.

Choose & share the list of GV’s required throughout the year.

Gvwala will share (annual) costing with agreement draft.

Post agreement is signed off, Corporate client can start placing an order by issuing PO & payment to us.

GVwala will deliver the E-GV’s, Gift Vouchers & Gift Cards within committed SLA to given location.

Our services are used by both corporates and direct customers.
